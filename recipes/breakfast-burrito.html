Breakfast Burrito

This delicious breakfast burrito with potatoes and chorizo will take your breakfast game up a notch. The rich flavors will have you coming back for more. Serve with a side of salsa, sour cream, and fruit.

Ingredients 1/2 pound fresh chorizo 1/2 poblano pepper, chopped 1/2 yellow onion, chopped 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped 2 Yukon gold potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces 4 large eggs 1/3 cup half-and-half salt and pepper to taste 2 tablespoons butter or olive oil 1/4 cup shredded cheese, any kind 4 burrito-sized flour tortillas
